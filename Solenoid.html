The Right-Hand Grip Rule

To determine the direction of the magnetic field in a solenoid:

  1. Imagine gripping the coil with your right hand.
  2. Let your fingers curl around the coil in the direction of the conventional current (positive to negative, indicated by the arrows).
  3. Your extended thumb will point in the direction of the magnetic field inside the coil, which is towards the North Pole (N).
