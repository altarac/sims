How to play:

  • Move your mouse/finger into the canvas area to use the selected tool.
  • Induction: Bring a charged rod near a neutral ball. It polarizes and attracts!
  • Conduction: Touch the ball with the rod to transfer charge. Watch them repel.
  • Grounding: Touch a charged ball with the Ground tool to discharge it.
