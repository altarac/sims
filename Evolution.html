How to Play

Welcome to the Evolution Simulator! You are the predator and the moving circles are your prey.

Your goal is to click on and "eat" as many circles as possible before the 20-second timer runs out. The surviving circles will reproduce and pass their colors on to the next generation. Watch as they evolve to camouflage against the background over time!

Color Evolution

Each bar represents the full population (40). Colors are stacked and sorted to show distribution.
