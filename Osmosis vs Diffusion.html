Membrane Dynamics Lab

A common mistake is confusing how water moves with how particles move. Use this split-screen lab to observe the fundamental difference between Diffusion (particles moving) and Osmosis (water moving).

Diffusion

No Membrane

Watch the dye particles spread from an area of high concentration to low concentration until evenly distributed.

Osmosis

Semi-Permeable

Salt particles cannot cross the membrane. Instead, water moves to the saltier side to balance the concentration.

Key Takeaway

Notice how the total volume in the Diffusion tank never changes—only the particles move around. In the Osmosis tank, because the salt is blocked by the membrane, the water is forced to shift sides. Osmosis is the diffusion of water!
