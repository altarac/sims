Compressibility

Explore how the state of matter affects compressibility. Observe the behavior of particles when external pressure is applied to the syringe.

PULL PUSH

Observation

Gas particles are spaced far apart and move freely. When force is applied, the empty space allows the particles to be squeezed much closer together, easily compressing the gas and increasing its internal pressure.
