How to Play

A Math Triangle shows how numbers are related in addition and subtraction families.

Top Vertex → The Whole (the largest value in addition)
WHOLE = PART + PART
Bottom Vertices → The two Parts
  1. Look at the equation (e.g. □ + 5 = 12).
  2. Find the largest value (the whole). In this case, 12.
  3. Drag the card containing the largest value to the Top Vertex.
  4. Drag the other two numbers (the parts, including the unknown □) to the bottom vertices.
  5. Use the keypad to enter the value of the unknown □ (e.g. 7).
